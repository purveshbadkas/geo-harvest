About GEO-Harvest

GEO-Harvest is your trusted platform for smarter farming decisions, offering real-time data and predictions to help farmers optimize their yields.

  • Predicts crop success based on state and crop name.
  • Provides market price predictions for crops in Maharashtra by entering district, market name, and crop.
  • Offers real-time updates on market prices and weather to keep farmers informed. Combines advanced technology with agriculture for better efficiency and profitability.

At GEO-Harvest, we aim to support farmers by providing easy-to-use tools that make farming more efficient and successful.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? Check out our frequently asked questions below to find quick answers..

1. How do I predict crop success rates?

Enter the state and crop name in our prediction tool, and you’ll get an instant forecast.

2. Can I check market prices for all states?

Currently, market price predictions are available for Maharashtra. Just enter the district, market name, and crop.

3. How accurate are the predictions?

We use advanced data analysis to provide reliable predictions, but weather and other factors can affect outcomes.

4. Is the weather data live?

Yes, we provide real-time weather updates for your location.

5. How do I contact support?

You can use the contact form on our website to reach us with any questions or feedback.
